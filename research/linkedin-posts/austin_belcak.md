### Summary

Austin shares the 5:50:5 framework, which encourages professionals to complete five job search activities, spend 50 minutes per day, and maintain consistency five days per week.

### Key Insights

- Systems create better results than random effort.
- Consistency compounds over time.
- Relationship building is a major driver of opportunities.

### Applications for B2B SaaS

- Create repeatable customer acquisition systems.
- Track daily growth activities.
- Prioritize relationships before sales conversations.
