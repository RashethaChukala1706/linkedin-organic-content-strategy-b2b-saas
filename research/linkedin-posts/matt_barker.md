# Key Lessons for B2B SaaS Companies

## 1. Build Category Ownership

Instead of saying:

> “We provide an AI sales platform.”

Create a recognizable idea:

> “We help sales teams eliminate manual prospect research.”

---

## 2. Turn Expertise Into Content

Use:

- Customer questions
- Product insights
- Industry lessons
- Founder experiences

as content sources.

---

## 3. Build Trust Before Selling

Matt’s approach shows that buyers often trust companies after repeated exposure to useful ideas.

SaaS companies should:

- Educate the market.
- Share customer outcomes.
- Explain problems clearly.
- Develop recognizable viewpoints.
