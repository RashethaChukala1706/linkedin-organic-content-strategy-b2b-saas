## Post 1: "It's absolutely exhausting to chase goals that aren't yours."

**Date:** June 2026

Link: [https://tinyurl.com/2nvsta2c](https://tinyurl.com/2nvsta2c)

### Summary

In this post, Justin highlights the importance of defining success on your own terms instead of following society's expectations. He explains that many professionals experience burnout because they spend years pursuing goals that don't align with their values and personal aspirations.

### Key Insights

- Success is different for everyone.
- Chasing someone else's definition of success leads to burnout.
- Personal and professional goals should align with individual values.

- Focus on meaningful growth instead of vanity metrics.
