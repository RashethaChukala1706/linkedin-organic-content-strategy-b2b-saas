## Key Points

1. Justin built a one-person business by focusing on leverage instead of employees.
2. Content distribution became a major growth engine.
3. A specific niche helps attract the right audience.
4. Personal expertise can become products and services.
5. Consistency compounds audience trust over time.

## Actionable Insights for B2B SaaS

- Build authority by sharing product and industry knowledge.
- Turn customer problems into educational content.
- Create a clear niche positioning.
- Use content as a long-term acquisition channel.

## Personal Takeaway

A strong personal brand is built by consistently sharing useful ideas with a specific audience and turning expertise into scalable assets.
