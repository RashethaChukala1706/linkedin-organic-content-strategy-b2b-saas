## Key Points

1. LinkedIn reach is strongly influenced by content quality and audience engagement.
2. Different post formats perform differently (polls, documents, text, images, video).
3. Watch time and dwell time are important signals for distribution.
4. Carousels can increase engagement because users spend more time consuming them.
5. Understanding algorithm behavior helps creators improve content strategy.

## Actionable Insights for B2B SaaS

- Test multiple LinkedIn content formats.
- Create educational carousel posts explaining industry insights.
- Optimize the first lines of posts to increase attention.
- Focus on meaningful comments instead of only likes.
- Track which content formats generate leads.

## Personal Takeaway

LinkedIn growth is not random. By understanding how the platform distributes content and creating posts that hold attention, professionals and companies can build stronger audiences.
