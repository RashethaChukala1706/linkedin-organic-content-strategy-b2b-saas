## Key Points

1. Customers do not buy information; they buy transformation and outcomes.
2. Understanding customer problems is the foundation of good marketing.
3. Market research helps create better offers.
4. Customer language should guide messaging.
5. Businesses should validate ideas before building products.

## Actionable Insights for B2B SaaS

- Interview customers before creating new features.
- Collect exact customer phrases for landing pages.
- Build messaging around customer problems.
- Validate demand before investing heavily.

## Personal Takeaway

Great marketing starts with understanding why people buy, not simply explaining what you sell.
